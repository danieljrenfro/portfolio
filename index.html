Intro

My name is Daniel Renfro. Welcome to my portfolio! I am a student in the Thinkful Engineering Immersion Program. Before this program, I worked as a Salesforce Admin. Wanting to have greater control over what I could create and provide to my end-users, I decided to pursue software development!

I'm located in San Antonio, TX. I love working and learning with others. If you are looking for someone to collaborate with or to connect locally, reach out to me via email.

About Me

Why Software Development?

I love to build. In software development, there are limitless possibilities and new things to learn. It's amazing how you can start with a blank screen and with some keystrokes build a website or application!

I love the aspect of encountering problems and issues and having to come up with creative solutions to overcome those obstacles. There is something about seeing broken tests all turn green.

You can't always solve a problem on your own. There is nothing like collaborating with your teammate and coming up with a solution you couldn't have found on your own.

By the end of the Thinkful program, I will have a working knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, React, NodeJS, and Express. After the program, I will be transitioning to a full-time software developer.
