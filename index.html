Intro

My name is Daniel Renfro. Welcome to my portfolio! I am a soon-to-be graduate from the Thinkful Engineering Immersion Program. Before Thinkful, I was a Salesforce Administrator. I wanted to have greater possibilities of the solutions I delivered, so I decided to pursue software development!

I'm located in San Antonio, TX. I love working and learning with others. If you are looking for someone to collaborate with or to connect locally, reach out to me via email.

About Me

Why Software Development?

I love to build. In software development, there are limitless possibilities and new things to learn. It's amazing how you can start with a blank screen and with some keystrokes build a website or application!

I love the challenge of encountering problems and issues and overcoming them with creative solutions. There is something about seeing broken tests all turn green.

You can't always solve a problem on your own. I thrive and enjoy collaborating with others towards a commmon goal and coming up with a solution you couldn't have found on your own.

I am skilled in HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, React.js, Node.js, and Express. After the program, I will be transitioning to a full-time software developer.
